اهلا وسهلا يا شباب!

Welcome to the House of Wisdom, Arabic students! This blog is a collection of resources meant for native English speakers who are learning the Arabic language. We have links to dictionary resources as well as entertaining videos and reading materials to help you practice comprehension. If you are just getting started with Arabic and are still learning the alphabet, these materials will likely be too advanced for you. Instead try out this learning tool. فرصة سعيدة!
